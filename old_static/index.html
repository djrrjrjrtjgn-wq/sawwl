How it Works

Delegate your SKR to a Guardian

Guardians are trusted node operators who verify Seeker devices and review dApp submissions. Delegation happens automatically when you stake.

Earn staking rewards

SKR inflation is paid out to stakers and compounds automatically to grow your position over time.

Unstake with a 48-hour cooldown

You can start unstaking anytime. After a 48-hour cooldown, your SKR is ready to withdraw back to your wallet.

FAQs

SKR staking lets you delegate your tokens to Guardians who help secure the Solana Mobile network. You earn rewards based on network inflation.

SKR inflation starts at 10% at TGE and decreases by 25% each year until reaching 2%. Your APY depends on current inflation and the total SKR staked.

Unstaking SKR takes 48 hours. During this cooldown period, your SKR is locked and does not earn staking rewards. After 48 hours, you must manually withdraw your SKR for it to appear in your wallet.

No. Once you unstake SKR, it stops earning staking rewards immediately, including during the 48-hour cooldown.

Yes. You can initiate additional unstaking while another amount is cooling down. However, the two unstake amounts will be combined and the cool down period will be reset to 48 hours.

Yes. You can cancel during cooldown and your SKR will be restaked automatically.
